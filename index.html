Format

The Project Checklist for the Hackathon of the Future of Work (see the above demo). 

1. Title
Short, powerful, and creative.

2. Figure
Eye-catching, both scientific and arty, all shapes and colors should serve for a purpose.

3. Legend
All functional shapes and colors need to be explained.

4. Significance
Why this visualization matters to our society?

5. Findings
What unknowns are turned into knowns?

6. Data
Any dataset found by searching "employment" or "work" as keywords on Kaggle.

7. Method
What methods are used to analyze the data?

8. Discussion
The big, inspiring questions remaining unsolved. 

*9. Story (optional)
Personal stories as the motivation to this research.
